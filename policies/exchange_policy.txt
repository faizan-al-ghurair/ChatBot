EXCHANGE & RETURN POLICY

Return Window:
Customers may return unused items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Items must be in original packaging with tags attached.

Exchange Process:
To exchange an item, contact our support team within 30 days. We will arrange a pickup and deliver the replacement item within 3-5 business days.

Non-Returnable Items:
The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:
- Undergarments and swimwear
- Personalized or custom made items
- Digital products and gift cards
- Sale items marked as final sale

Refund Timeline:
Refunds are processed within 7 business days after we receive the returned item. Amount is credited back to the original payment method.

Damaged Items:
If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact us within 48 hours with photos. We will arrange an immediate replacement at no cost.

Shipping Costs:
Return shipping is free for defective items. For other returns, a shipping fee of AED 20 will be deducted from your refund.
